Append the data for a gcode cylinder to input vectors.

A gcode cylinder is a cylinder with the arcs from PI/4 to 3PI/4 and 5PI/4 to 7PI/4 replaced with flat lines. It looks like a cylinder that has been squished flat on two sides. This is the public interface to the gcode graphics which translates the start and displacement into a transform matrix usable by the private function of the same name.

Parameters
widthRadius of gcode cylinder
heightHeight of gcode cylinder
startVector indicating where the center of the base of this cylinder should be
displacementVector indicating the direction and length of this cylinder
colorColor of gcode cylinder
verticesVector of vertices to append the new vertices to
colorsVector of colors to append the new colors to
normalsVector of normals to append the new normals to
